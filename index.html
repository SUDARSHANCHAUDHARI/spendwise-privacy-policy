Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

SpendWise ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SpendWise mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

SpendWise is a personal finance app. All financial data you enter — expenses, categories, budgets, and receipt photos — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit this data to any server.

  • Expense records — amounts, categories, notes, and dates you enter manually
  • Receipt photos — images captured via camera for expense documentation
  • Budget settings — spending limits and category configurations

Information Collected Automatically

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera (CAMERA) — used to photograph receipts for expense tracking. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded.
  • Biometric authentication (USE_BIOMETRIC) — used to lock the App with fingerprint or face recognition. Your biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure hardware and is never accessed or stored by SpendWise.
  • Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — used on older Android versions (Android 9 and below / Android 12 and below) for saving and reading receipt images. On Android 13+, this permission is not required.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — used to send local budget alerts and reminders. These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve any external server.

On-Device OCR Processing

SpendWise uses Google ML Kit's Text Recognition to extract amounts from receipt photos. This processing happens entirely on your device — no receipt images or text are sent to Google or any third party.

On-Device Data Storage

All your financial data is stored in an encrypted local database (SQLCipher) and encrypted preferences on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g., via CSV export).

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To display and manage your personal expense records within the App
  • To generate spending reports and charts from your financial data
  • To send local budget alert notifications when you approach spending limits
  • To secure the App with biometric authentication
  • To scan receipt text via on-device OCR for faster expense entry

Third-Party Services

SpendWise does not use any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting services that transmit your data to third parties.

The only third-party component used is Google ML Kit Text Recognition, which runs entirely on-device and does not send any data to Google servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third parties. All data remains on your device.

The only scenario in which your data leaves the device is when you explicitly choose to export it (e.g., exporting a CSV file) or back it up via Android's standard backup mechanism.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App or using the in-app data deletion option. If you wish to request assistance with data deletion, contact us at sudarshantechlabs@gmail.com.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All financial data is stored in an AES-256 encrypted database (SQLCipher)
  • App settings and preferences are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Biometric data is handled by your device's Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) — SpendWise never has access to your raw biometric data
  • No network transmission of your financial data occurs

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at sudarshantechlabs@gmail.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the App (it is stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data at any time by clearing the App's data in Android Settings or uninstalling the App
  • Export your data in CSV format at any time from within the App

To exercise these rights or for any questions, contact us at sudarshantechlabs@gmail.com.

EEA and GDPR

Residents of the European Economic Area (EEA) have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since SpendWise stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to any server, the data processing that occurs is limited to what is necessary to provide the App's core functionality (legal basis: contract / legitimate interest).

You may contact us at sudarshantechlabs@gmail.com to exercise your rights of access, correction, deletion, or portability.

California (CCPA)

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell your personal information.
